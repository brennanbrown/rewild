# Session 6: Reflection and Futures

**Theme:** Integrating learning, acknowledging grief, and imagining next steps.

## Location ideas

- Return to a previously used location to notice how perception has changed.

## Environmental attunement (1015 minutes)

- Ask participants to compare their first impressions of this place to how they experience it now.

## Practices

- [Writing with the Cycles](/practices/writing-with-the-cycles/) focused on their semester-long work.
- Reflective letter to self or future students about ecological writing and responsibility.

## Reflection

- How has your sense of your own writing practice changed?
- What responsibilities or opportunities do you feel in relation to the places you write with?
